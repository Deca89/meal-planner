Quick and Easy Southwestern Corn Chowder

Nutrition

Calories: 313k

Fat: 11g

Carbohydrates: 39g

Protein: 18g

Gluten free: True

Dairy free: False

Ingredients

  • 2.0 Tb butter
  • 59.147 ml cheddar cheese
  • 1.0 tsp chili flakes
  • 907.185 g corn kernels
  • 118.294 ml green bell pepper
  • 59.147 ml half and half
  • 118.294 ml red bell pepper
  • 6.0 servings salt and pepper
  • 473.176 ml shredded chicken
  • 2.0 large sweet onions
  • 946.352 ml vegetable stock

Instructions

  1. In a Dutch oven, sweat the onions and peppers with a pinch of salt until onions are translucent.
  2. Add the chili flakes.
  3. Add the chicken stock, corn and chicken.
  4. Simmer covered for 10 minutes.
  5. Add the butter, half and half, and cheese, if desired.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste.
