BLT Sandwich

Nutrition

Calories: 679k

Fat: 60g

Carbohydrates: 12g

Protein: 20g

Gluten free: False

Dairy free: True

Ingredients

  • 2.0 servings bell pepper
  • 4.0 bread
  • 2.0 servings lettuce
  • 2.0 servings mayonnaise
  • 8.0 thick-cut bacon
  • 1.0 tomato

Instructions

  1. Brown bacon in a skillet
  2. Remove and pat off excess oil
  3. Slice tomato into 1/4 inches slices
  4. Toast bread
  5. Spread a thin layer of mayonnaise on bread
  6. Layer all ingredients on bread and close sandwich
  7. Add fresh cracked black pepper
