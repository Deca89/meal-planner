Tomato and lentil soup

Nutrition

Calories: 358k

Fat: 8g

Carbohydrates: 54g

Protein: 19g

Gluten free: True

Dairy free: True

Ingredients

  • 1.0 bay leaf
  • 480.0 g canned tomatoes
  • 3.0 carrots
  • 2.0 cloves garlic
  • 250.0 g lentils
  • 2.0 Tbs olive oil
  • 1.0 onion
  • 1.0 bunch parsley
  • 0.25 tsps salt
  • 1.3 l water

Instructions

Saut onion and garlic in olive oil for 5 minutes. Add the carrot, saut for another 2 minutes. Add tomatoes, bay leaf and water, stir and bring to the boil. Stir in lentils, season with salt and cook for 5 minutes. Before serving sprinkle with chopped parsley.
