Easy Gluten Free Vegetarian Pasta Salad

Nutrition

Calories: 567k

Fat: 8g

Carbohydrates: 106g

Protein: 16g

Gluten free: False

Dairy free: False

Ingredients

  • 0.25 tsps black pepper
  • 59.147 ml fresh cilantro
  • 453.592 g grapes
  • 2.0 Tbsps olive oil
  • 2.0 tsps orange juice
  • 1.0 tsp orange zest
  • 453.592 g pasta
  • 2.0 Tbsps plain greek yogurt
  • 0.25 tsps sea salt

Instructions

Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside to cool for 10 minutes. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients; gently toss to combine. Transfer pasta salad to four serving bowls. Serve chilled.
