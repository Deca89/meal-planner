Chinese Chicken Salad With Chipotle Dressing

Nutrition

Calories: 795k

Fat: 56g

Carbohydrates: 32g

Protein: 45g

Gluten free: True

Dairy free: True

Ingredients

  • 1.0 Tbsp almond butter
  • 118.294 ml canola oil
  • 2.0 carrots
  • 1.0 serving chili oil
  • 1.0 tsp chipotle pepper in adobo
  • 2.0 tsps chipotle pepper in adobo
  • 1.0 serving cow pea
  • 59.147 ml fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1.0 Tbsp fresh ginger
  • 59.147 ml fresh mint leaves
  • 59.147 ml green onion
  • 2.0 Tbsps honey
  • 1.0 halves lime
  • 0.5 head napa cabbage
  • 59.147 ml rice wine vinegar
  • 118.294 ml roasted peanuts
  • 0.5 head romaine lettuce
  • 473.176 ml rotisserie chicken
  • 1.0 serving salad mix
  • 1.0 serving salt and pepper
  • 2.0 tsps sesame oil
  • 1.0 Tbsp smooth peanut butter
  • 113.398 g snow peas
  • 1.0 Tbsp soy sauce

Instructions

Dressing: Whisk together the vinegar, peanut & almond butters, ginger, chipotle pepper puree, soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, and canola oil in a medium bowl. Season with salt and pepper, to taste. Salad: Combine cabbage, lettuce, carrots, snow peas, cilantro, and green onion in a large bowl. Add the dressing and toss to combine. Transfer to a serving platter and top with the shredded chicken, chopped peanuts, and mint. Drizzle with chili oil, if desired. Garnish with lime halves.
