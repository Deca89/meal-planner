Jade Buddha Salmon Tartare

Nutrition

Calories: 320k

Fat: 17g

Carbohydrates: 5g

Protein: 34g

Gluten free: True

Dairy free: True

Ingredients

  • 1.0 Tbsp chives
  • 1.0 Tbsp cilantro
  • 2.0 tsps ginger
  • 2.0 Tbsps green onion
  • 1.0 serving habanero chile
  • 1.0 Tbsp lime juice
  • 1.0 lime zest
  • 1.0 serving olive oil
  • 1.0 tsp rice vinegar
  • 340.194 g salmon
  • 1.0 serving salt and pepper
  • 1.0 Tbsp thai basil

Instructions

Combine all ingredients except lime juice and vinegar. Chill for 30 minutes. Add lime juice and vinegar, adjust to perfect, and serve.
