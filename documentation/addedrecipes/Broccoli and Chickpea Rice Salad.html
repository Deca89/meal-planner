Broccoli and Chickpea Rice Salad

Nutrition

Calories: 524k

Fat: 12g

Carbohydrates: 89g

Protein: 18g

Gluten free: True

Dairy free: True

Ingredients

  • 59.147 ml almonds
  • 946.352 ml broccoli florets
  • 473.176 ml brown rice
  • 2.0 Tbsps dijon mustard
  • 354.882 ml dried chickpeas
  • 59.147 ml fresh parsley
  • 1.0 clove garlic
  • 2.0 green onions
  • 6.0 servings ground pepper
  • 1.5 juice of lemon
  • 1.0 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2.0 tsps olive oil
  • 2.0 pineapple juice
  • 0.5 tsps red pepper flakes
  • 1.5 tsps sea salt
  • 78.863 ml sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2.0 tsps tamari soy sauce

Instructions

In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the broccoli, stir well, and cover. Cook, stirring often, until the broccoli is tender. Add the broccoli to a large bowl, along with the remaining salad ingredients.To make the dressing, whisk together all of the dressing ingredients in a small bowl. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well to coat evenly. Taste for seasoning and serve.
