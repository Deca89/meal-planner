Key Lime Pie

Nutrition

Calories: 763k

Fat: 27g

Carbohydrates: 117g

Protein: 16g

Gluten free: False

Dairy free: False

Ingredients

  • 5.0 servings graham cracker crust
  • 6.0 juice of lime
  • 1.0 Tbsp lime zest
  • 118.294 ml sour cream
  • 709.764 ml sweetened condensed milk
  • 5.0 servings whipped cream

Instructions

Heat oven to 350.Mix your lime juice, condensed milk, sour cream, and lime zest in a medium bowl until creamed together well.Pour mixture into the graham cracker crust.Bake for 6 - 8 minutes in the oven, making sure that you do not brown the top of the pie.Pull your pie from the oven and allow to cool to room temp.Place in the refrigerator and allow to completely cool.When you are ready, add your whipped cream and top it with a little additional zest and serve.
