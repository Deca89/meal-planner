Easy Slow Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup

Nutrition

Calories: 584k

Fat: 6g

Carbohydrates: 89g

Protein: 47g

Gluten free: True

Dairy free: True

Ingredients

  • 425.243 g black beans
  • 907.185 g chicken breast
  • 680.389 g chicken broth
  • 2.0 tsps chili powder
  • 113.398 g chilis
  • 59.147 ml cilantro
  • 425.243 g kernel corn
  • 1.0 medium onion
  • 793.787 g tomatoes

Instructions

Add all of your ingredients into the slow cooker and then cook on low for 6 hours. Once finished cooking, scoop into serving bowls and serve with tortilla chips, sour cream, cheese, avocado, or any of your favorite toppings.
