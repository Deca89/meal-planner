Easy Shrimp Scampi

Nutrition

Calories: 261k

Fat: 15g

Carbohydrates: 3g

Protein: 23g

Gluten free: True

Dairy free: False

Ingredients

  • 4.0 Tbsps butter
  • 2.0 Tbsps fresh parsley
  • 6.0 cloves garlic
  • 2.0 Tbsps lemon juice
  • 1.0 Tbsp olive oil
  • 0.25 tsps pepper
  • 0.5 tsps salt
  • 453.592 g shrimp
  • 118.294 ml white wine

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, melt butter and oil. Add garlic and saute 30 seconds.
  2. Stir in wine and lemon juice and cook 1 minute.
  3. Stir in shrimp, parsley, salt and pepper. Cook 2-3 minutes over high heat, stir constantly.
  4. Serve with pasta or rice.
